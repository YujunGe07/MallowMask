How It Works

Morningmallow combines three gentle sensory cues to wake you up naturally and quietly

  • - Soft internal LEDs mimic sunrise light and fade in slowly to signal your brain it's time to wake.
  • - Temple vibration motors buzz gently to boost alertness
  • - Thermal rings warm or cool your face to regulate your sleep cycle.

All of this is packed into a breathable, adjustable eye mask
